New Leap Initiative

New Leap Initiative is the R&D team of K J Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology. The team aims to promote space tech and Product Development skills among students. The team also has a registered Amateur Radio Club (viz. C. R.O.W.N) "Club for Radio Operations and Wireless Networks". The club aims to promote Amateur radio activities with the help of Amateur radio community among the students of various institutions.

About Us
